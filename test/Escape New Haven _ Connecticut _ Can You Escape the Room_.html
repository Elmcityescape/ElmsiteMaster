Can YOU Escape the Room?

Located at 103 Whitney Ave, Escape New Haven is a cooperative, real-life “escape room” — an adventure space where nothing is as it seems. You and your team are trapped; you have 60 minutes to escape a room by solving a series of puzzles that will challenge your mind and confound your senses. Explore, adapt, work together, think creatively — and you just might succeed. Learn More…

Copyright © 2016 Escape Industries, LLC, New Haven, CT. Our venue is accessible from just about anywhere in Connecticut -- Hartford, Stamford, Bridgeport, Waterbury, Norwalk, Danbury, New Britain, Bristol, Meriden, Milford, Hamden, West Haven, Middletown, etc -- and we also welcome visitors from anywhere else, near or far. If you're traveling, try our sister locations: Escape Rhode Island and Escape Sacramento.
